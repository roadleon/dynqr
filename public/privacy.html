Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 01, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how A. Logos Ailys ("the Operator") handles information in connection with the dynamic QR code generation service "DynQR" ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you create a dynamic QR code, we collect and store the following information in our database (Supabase):

  • Destination URL: The URL you provide for the QR code to redirect to.
  • Generated Short Code and Edit Token: Randomly generated strings used to identify and allow editing of your QR code link.

We do not require user registration and do not collect any personal identification information such as name, email address, or IP address.

2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the Service, which is to redirect QR codes to the destination URL you set.
  • To allow you to update the destination URL using your secret edit token.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a Supabase project managed by the Operator. We rely on the security measures provided by Supabase to protect the data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Deletion

We currently do not offer a feature for users to delete their generated links. The data will be retained indefinitely unless the service is discontinued. We may delete data that is associated with prohibited acts or is deemed malicious.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: contact-qr@ailys.app
